Connect4AI
In August 2020, I decided that I wanted to gain more experience working with full stack applications, so I began working on a project called Connect4AI. Connect4AI is a flask web app that I built entirely from scratch, learning flask throughout the process. This web app allows users to play the popular game Connect4 against another user on the same device, or against an AI. The AI relies on the minimax algorithm with alpha-beta pruning in order to decide its moves, with various search depths resulting in various difficulties. I am also in the process of implementing a leaderboard feature which will rely on a Postgres database, which will rank players based on the following factors (for games that they win): AI difficulty, number of moves, amount of time taken.

S.O.I.S. (Support Our International Students)
On July 6th, 2020, the Trump Administration announced a policy that would force international students that were not enrolled in in-person classes for the upcoming Fall Semester to leave the country. The following day, my friend McClain and I began working on a website that provided resources to international students and connected them with domestic students in order to potentially swap into their in-person classes. I worked on the backend of this site, using my experience with Google Apps Script to turn a popular Google Sheet into a more secure, accessible, and readable site. This site was up and running in less than 60 hours of its inception, and it had over 5000 hits within just 12 hours of its launch.
